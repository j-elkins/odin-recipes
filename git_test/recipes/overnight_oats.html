Chia Almond Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup old fashioned oats
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 Tbps chia seeds
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1/2 cup berries
  • 1 Tbsp chopped nuts

Instructions

  1. In a pint jar or resealable container, add the milk, oats, yogurt, chia seeds and honey.
  2. Mix thoroughly, seal up and refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  3. In the morning or when ready to eat, stir in the raspberries and almonds. Enjoy!
